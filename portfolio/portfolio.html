Who are we

Elvyn Richards Hall is a student residence located in Loughborough, a town in the East Midlands region of England. The hall is part of Loughborough University's accommodation offering and provides a range of living arrangements for students, including en-suite rooms, shared flats, and studios. The residence is situated within walking distance of the university's main campus and offers a variety of amenities, including a common room, laundry facilities, and an on-site gym. Additionally, Elvyn Richards Hall is staffed 24/7 by a team of friendly and helpful personnel who are available to provide support and assistance to residents as needed. Overall, Elvyn Richards Hall provides a safe and comfortable living environment for students studying at Loughborough University.
